What is the Fate Universe?

The Fate franchise is essentially one big multiverse that has various timelines that lead it to its unique style of narration. Fate mainly revolves around the holy grail war which is essentially various people summoning servants of history from the past, present or future to fight and obtain the holy grail. The holy grail is a wish granting device that randomly appears after winning the war. Fate can be watched, read or even played in any order because it is essentially one big timeline of events. Of course there are other timelines that diverge from the original such as Fate/Prisma Illya which is a universe in where instead of servants they are given class cards that turns masters into that historical figure with a magical girl kind of vibe.

What are Servants?

Servants are historical figures called upon by the grail to participate in a holy grail war and help the servants that are participating to win the grail.

Types of Servants

There are mainly 7 types of servants in the Fate universe. These servants are Saber the masters of the swords, Lancer the masters of the spear, Archer the masters of Long Range Weaponry, Caster the masters of various magic and mystic arts, Rider the servants with the mounts, Assasin the master of espionage and stealh and finally Berserker the servants that turned mad due to curses or spells.
