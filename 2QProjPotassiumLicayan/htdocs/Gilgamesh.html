Gilgamesh

Background

Gilgamesh is a half-god, half-human king. He was known as the King of Heroes who possessed all things in the world, whose tale is recorded in mankind's oldest epic poem, the Epic of Gilgamesh. He is mankind's oldest hero and the origin of all the tales and myths of different heroes around the world.
