Shirou Emiya

Background:

Shirou was found and adopted by Kiritsugu Emiya after the fourth holy grail war. During the war, the great fire occured around Fuyuki City and Shirou was mortally wounded leaving Kiritsugu no choice but to insert Avalon inside Shirou's body. Five years after the war, Kiritsugu passes away leaving Shirou Emiya alone. Kiritsugu passed on his philosophy of wishing to be a Hero of Justice capable of saving anyone to Shirou.
